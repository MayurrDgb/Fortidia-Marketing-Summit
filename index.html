THE AI SPECTRUM
The AI tools landscape
Each tool has a role. Understanding them is the first step to using them well.
GENERAL PURPOSE
ChatGPT
OpenAI · Most widely adopted
Writing, brainstorming, translation, data analysis, image generation — a versatile everyday assistant.
Image output quality varies — not always print-ready for large formats.
Best for: everyday content & ideation
WORKSPACE INTEGRATED
Gemini
Google · Native to our environment
Embedded in Docs, Slides, Gmail, Meet. Strong for summarisation and workspace productivity tasks.
Image generation is limited — AI-generated visuals are often visibly artificial, unsuitable for marketing.
Best for: internal productivity & docs
REASONING & ENTERPRISE
Claude
Anthropic · Enterprise-grade
Deep reasoning, long-form content, complex briefs, structured outputs, and agentic task execution.
Designed for workflows where accuracy, nuance, and multi-step thinking matter most.
Best for: strategy, automation & complex tasks
FULL AI STACK
AI + Automation
Orchestrated · Brand-safe · Scalable
AI alone isn't enough. Connecting AI to automation layers and branded visual engines unlocks production at scale.
Brand identity — logos, colours, typography — stays protected by design.
Best for: campaigns, assets & workflows
Key insight —
AI tools are complementary, not competing. The value comes from knowing when to use which — and how to connect them.
